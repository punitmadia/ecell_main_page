Our Initiatives

E-Summit

The Entrepreneurship Summit is our annual flagship event which is a confluence of visionary students, professionals or anybody who has a passion for leaving a dent in the universe, start his own business or fund a venture and become a trendsetter.

Eureka

Eureka! is Asia's largest B-Model competition which aims at encouraging people from across the globe to flesh out their ideas and form global enterprises that combine technology, vision and business acumen.

NEC

National Entrepreneurship Challenge's mission is to help other colleges create an ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs by establishing an E-Cell in their respective institutes.

SSP

Startup Services Platform provides some of the most necessary services availed by startups at concessional rates as compared to market rates.

Fincof

Freelancers, Interns & Co-Founders platform connects startupsto students who are interested in the kind of work they have to offer & bring their idea to life.

EnB

Entrepreneurship & Business club is a community where innovative ideas are born & nurtured into successful business ventures. It is the face of E-cell inside IIT Bombay.

Embracing Autism

Embracing Autism was the Social Initiative taken by Ecell IIT Bombay to promote awareness and knowledge about the spectrum of abilities that grows within these autistic individuals.

EnSpace

An official Entrepreneurship Learning Platform by E-cell, IIT Bombay.

Eureka Jr.

Eureka! Junior provides a platform to present their business idea. It is a competition that focuses to develop the entrepreneurial mindset of a student at an early stage.

Highlights

Mentors Meet

Mentor Meetup was a networking event to help connecting entrepreneurs and enthusiasts to investors, mentors and market leaders all across the country. Here, attendees had ono-on-one mentoring and CNT (Common Networking Time) where they interacted with different startups at the event.

JEH

Are you one of those who are still confused between what to pursue in their future. Jobs vs Entrepreneurship vs Higher Education is a way to resolve what to do in your future. With Every field having its own pros and cons, deciding the correct future is very tough job for every student. Come and join us in a discussion which might help you decide what future you want to pursue.
